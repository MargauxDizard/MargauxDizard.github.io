Diploma project aimed at enhancing the image of the toy library. The Paris City Hall is inaugurating several toy libraries and wishes to communicate on the event. Imagination of an interactive campaign: 4 moving posters are to be found in the city, to recreate the message: what? when? or? for whom? The campaign takes the form of a treasure hunt. Each poster features an interactive mini game that, like the game library, allowing you to reconnect with the materiality of manual play.

2019-Code and design by Margaux Dizard
