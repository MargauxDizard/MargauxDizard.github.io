I propose a sort of brief escape to the Montreuil users of the subway, just for a second. To highlight the diversity of the city, with an app,, the user will be able to share with others what best represents the city (an objet, a colour, a text). Then from all the collected answers , a poster will be generated . It symbolises the mixity you can find in Montreuil. An unusual collage appears, a mix between a ready-made and a dada collage, with a dreamlike and sweetened background. It contrasts with the hand-made and popular aethetics of the daily-life objects.
