Project based on generative design. From Georges Perec’s protocole, which generates basic sentences you can find on postcards («243 cartes postales en couleurs véritables»). I imagined my own instructions for use, to illustrate these cards. Work under duress, the result reminds us of an amateur and vernacular style.

2019-Code and design by Margaux Dizard
